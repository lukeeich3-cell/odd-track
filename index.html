How to play

  1. Host enters player names and two songs.
  2. App secretly gives 4 people the main song and 1 person the odd song.
  3. Everyone opens their own private link on their phone.
  4. Headphones on. Hit play. Dance.
  5. Vote for who looks wrong. Then reveal.

Best version without logins or an App Store: the host creates one round, then shares private links. If you only want one group-chat link, use the “group join link,” but friends could technically peek if they try hard.
